Features you will
love & enjoy

Architects do more than design. They dream, and they do. If you are considering a career in architecture, be prepared to do more than simply design buildings. You'll need to be able to juggle angles, lines and numbers.

Killer negotiation skills

Believe it or not, but architects must be sociable. They must be able to clearly communicate with their clients as well as the interior designers, engineers and staff with whom they work.

Love to learn

In order to keep working as an architect, you must be able to commit to continuing your education. You will need become licensed in the state in which you want to work, and this process usually involves education.

Know things

The field of architecture isn't limited to just drawing and design. Architects have knowledge of an array of topics that might not necessarily be related to the profession such as politics and public speaking. In addition, a knowledge of construction.

Love nature

Yes, architecture revolves around science and mathematics, but architects also often have a deep respect and profound love of nature. Frank Lloyd Wright believed that architects' designs should never disrupt Mother Nature.
